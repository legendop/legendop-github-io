Noise cancelling headphone are the future in this fast developing world and unwanted noise present everywhere around us.

Types of Noise Cancellation

In this fast developing world, noise is present everywhere. And hardly a person gets a time free of noise. There comes the need for noise cancelling headphones. They basically have two methods to cancel noise - Passive noise cancelling and Active noise cancelling.

Passive Noise cancelling

As the name suggests, it is a passive way of noise cancelling. No technology is required in this process, hence no power consumption. It simply blocks or prevents the outer noise or sound to enter in our ears. The efficiency of it solely depends upon the sturucture of the headphone and materials used. Earbuds which fit perfectly in our ears are more efficient in preventing/blocking noise, that's why companies provide earphone with earbuds of many sizes. The other thing that effects this is the material used - rubber earbuds are way more comfortable and fit perfectly in our ears than the plastic ones.

So, be careful while purchasing noise cancelling headphones.

Active Noise Cancellation

This is the main technology in noise cancelling earphones. A noise is created which cancels the noise coming from outside. This technology has been there from a very long time. In the airplane there was unwanted noise due to turbulance, so the headphones the pilots used had this technology for noise free communication between pilots and air control. So, how is the perfect anti noise created?

There are microphones outside each of the earbuds. They pic up the noise and the software instantly creates an anti-noise for it having 180 degree phase difference. This sound is played in the headphones. It makes us feel that there is no noise, but actually 2 sounds are there which cancel each other. As the sound travels slower than electric signal, there is enough time for the software to create a noise in opposite polarity and play it in the earphones at the same time the outer noise travels from outside of the earbud to inside our ears.

This technology is not practicable in traditional earphones as we hear different noise in both our ears and there is only one microphone in traditional earphones and also far from our ears. That's why inside of cancelling noise, more unwanted noise is produced.

Conclusion

Noise cancelling headphones how they work? Noise cancelling technology is present in only few headphones and they are more costlier than normal headphones. But they are worth it for people who need to travel a lot for their offices or colleges and also for people who don't need and distraction listening to their favourite music in loop.

These headphone are also capable of recording more clear voice aa they have more microphones and unwanted noise can also be removed with proper software. Their working is just creating an anti-noise to cancel the outer noise, giving a noise free environment to the listener
